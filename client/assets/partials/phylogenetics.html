Phylogenetic Tree Comparison

The following UPGMA phylogeny was genereated by calculating a fixed distance value(# of changes / length of each pair). The frequencies were then mapped using a model-based distance approach with the implementation of a Jukes Contor correction.

Amino Acid Mutation Frequency Synonymous Mutation = Excess of synonymous mutations points towards negative purifying selection.

Non-Synonymous Mutation = Excess of non-synonymous mutations points towards positive diversifying selection.
